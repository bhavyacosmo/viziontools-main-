Procurement Efficiency Improvement

An SMB manufacturer in Northern India after Vizion Tools deployed Odoo procurement and inventory modules. No new staff. Purchase cycle time dropped, and duplicate orders stopped.

Most manufacturers don't have a bad team.
They have a bad system architecture.

Production runs on tribal knowledge held by two people. Procurement happens through WhatsApp threads that nobody can audit. Inventory lives in a spreadsheet that's always slightly wrong. And finance sits completely separate from operations — which means the P&L tells you what happened, never what's about to.

The result: machines run when they shouldn't, materials are ordered twice (or not at all), and the cost of a production run isn't known until the job is closed. Sometimes weeks later.

A 60% procurement efficiency gain is achievable — not by hiring more buyers, but by removing the manual steps that consume the process.

The tools exist. The question is whether they're configured to match your floor — or just installed and left alone.
